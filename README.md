# Nexteach 360 Interactive ESL Games

Welcome to Nexteach 360 Interactive ESL Games - a collection of engaging, interactive games designed to help students learn English as a Second Language (ESL).

## Features

- **Word Match Game**: Match words with their definitions to test vocabulary knowledge
- **Vocabulary Quiz**: Multiple-choice quiz to test understanding of English words and meanings
- **Sentence Builder**: Build correct sentences by arranging words in the proper order

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Choose a game from the main menu
3. Follow the on-screen instructions for each game
4. Track your score and improve your English skills!

## Games Description

### Word Match
Match English words with their correct definitions. Click on a word, then click on its matching definition. Correct matches turn green, incorrect ones shake and turn red. Complete all matches to win!

### Vocabulary Quiz
Answer multiple-choice questions about English vocabulary. Select the correct answer from the options provided. Your score is displayed at the end.

### Sentence Builder
Arrange words in the correct order to form proper English sentences. Click words from the word bank to build your sentence, then check your answer. Words can be removed by clicking them in the sentence area.
